Andrej Dujella:

Algorithmic Aspects of Elliptic Curves


Faculty of Informatics, University of Debrecen, May 2007

Contents

Elliptic curves over Q: Group law. Computing Mordell-Weil group. Construction of curves with high rank.

Elliptic curves over finite fields: Implementation of operations. Algorithms for determining the group order.

Elliptic Curve Cryptography: Public key cryptography based on discrete logarithm problem. Menezes-Vanstone and ECDSA cryptosystems. Elliptic curve discrete logarithm problem. Comparing ECC with other types of cryptography.

Other applications of elliptic curves: Lenstra's elliptic curve factoring method. Elliptic curve primality proving algorithm.
