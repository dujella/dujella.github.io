3.11 Exercises

Concering Exercise 12 on page 166, let us mention that in the recent paper:
A. Dujella, M. Kazalicki, V. Petričević, Rational Diophantine sextuples with strong pair, Rev. R. Acad. Cienc. Exactas Fis. Nat. Ser. A Math. RACSAM 119, Article 36 (2025),
it is shown that there exist infinitely many rational Diophantine sextuples {a,b,c,d,e,f} with the property that a2+1 and b2+1 are perfect squares.
