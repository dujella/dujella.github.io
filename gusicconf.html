Workshop on Number Theory and Algebra

on the occasion of 60th birthday of Ivica Gusić

November 26 - 28, 2014, Zagreb, Croatia

Organized by Seminar on Number Theory and Algebra and
Department of Mathematics, Faculty of Science, University of Zagreb

Financially supported by Croatian Science Foundation under the project no. 6422

The conference will be held at Department of Mathematics, Bijenička cesta 30.
All talks will take place in room A101 on the first floor.
