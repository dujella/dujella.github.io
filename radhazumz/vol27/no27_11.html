NUMERICAL RADIUS POINTS OF A BILINEAR MAPPING FROM THE PLANE WITH THE l1-NORM INTO ITSELF

Sung Guen Kim

Department of Mathematics, Kyungpook National University, Daegu 702-701, Republic of Korea
e-mail: sgk317@knu.ac.kr

Abstract.   For n ≥ 2 and a Banach space E we let Π(E) = {[x*, x1, . . . , xn] : x*(xj) = ∥x*∥ = ∥xj∥ = 1 for j = 1, . . . , n}. Let L(nE : E) denote the space of all continuous n-linear mappings from E to itself. An element [x*, x1, . . . , xn] ∈ Π(E) is called a numerical radius point of T ∈ L(nE : E) if |x*(T(x1, . . . , xn))| = v(T), where v(T) is the numerical radius of T. Nradius(T) denotes the set of all numerical radius points of T. In this paper we classify Nradius(T) for every T ∈ L(2l12 : l12) in connection with Norm(T), where Norm(T) denotes the set of all norming points of T.
