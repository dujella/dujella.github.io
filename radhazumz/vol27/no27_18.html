THE (LARGEST) LEBESGUE NUMBER AND ITS RELATIVE VERSION/h3>

Vera Tonić

Department of Mathematics, University of Rijeka, 51 000 Rijeka, Croatia
e-mail: vera.tonic@math.uniri.hr

Abstract.   In this paper we compare different definitions of the (largest) Lebesgue number of a cover U for a metric space X. We also introduce the relative version for the Lebesgue number of a covering family U for a subset A ⊆ X, and justify the relevance of introducing it by giving a corrected statement and proof of the Lemma 3.4 from the paper by Buyalo and Lebedeva (2007), involving λ-quasi homothetic maps with coefficient R between metric spaces and the comparison of the mesh and the Lebesgue number of a covering family for a subset on both sides of the map.
