β-EXPANSION OF p-ADIC NUMBERS WITH PISOT-CHABAUTY BASES

Marwa Ghorbel, Rim Ghorbel and Mohamed Hbaib

Faculté des Sciences de Sfax, Département de Mathematiques, BP 1171, Sfax 3000, Tunisia
e-mail: marwaghorbel123@gmail.com
e-mail: rimaghorbel@yahoo.fr
e-mail: mohamed.hbaieb@fss.usf.tn

Abstract.   The aim of this paper is to study some arithmetic properties about the finiteness and the periodicity of the β-expansion of p-adic numbers. We prove that if the β-expansion of unity or rationals verifies some conditions then β is a Pisot-Chabauty number.

2020 Mathematics Subject Classification.   11R06, 37B50.

Key words and phrases.   p-adic numbers, β-expansion, PC number.
