GENERALIZED HERMITE-HADAMARD INEQUALITIES FOR (α, η, γ, δ)-p CONVEX FUNCTIONS

Muhammad Bilal, Silvestru Sever Dragomir and Asif Raza Khan

Abstract.   In this article, we would like to introduce another generalized class of convex functions which we call as (α, η, γ, δ)-p convex functions. This new class contains another two new classes namely, (α, η)-p convex functions of the 1st and 2nd kinds. Further, we also generalize some results related to famous Hermite-Hadamard type inequality stated in [2] for the aforementioned class of functions with distinct techniques. Hence various existed and new results would be captured as special case of our obtained results. Moreover, application to midpoint formula has also been established.
