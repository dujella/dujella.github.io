Rad HAZU, Matematičke znanosti, Vol. 21 (2017), 89-98.

TWO SIMPLE OBSERVATIONS ON REPRESENTATIONS OF METAPLECTIC GROUPS

Marko Tadić

Department of Mathematics, Faculty of Science, University of Zagreb, 10000 Zagreb, Croatia
e-mail: tadic@math.hr

Abstract.   M. Hanzer and I. Matić have proved that the genuine unitary principal series representations of the metaplectic groups are irreducible. A simple consequence of that paper is a criterion for the irreducibility of the non-unitary principal series representations of the metaplectic groups that we give in this paper.

2010 Mathematics Subject Classification.   Primary: 22D12, Secondary: 22E50, 22D30, 11F85.

Key words and phrases.   Metaplectic groups, non-archimedean local fields, parabolic induction, principal series representations, irreducibility.
