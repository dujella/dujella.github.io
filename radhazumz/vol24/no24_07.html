SOME WEIGHTED TRAPEZOIDAL TYPE INEQUALITIES VIA h-PREINVEXITY

B. Meftah and K. Mekalfa

Laboratoire des télécommunications, Faculté des Sciences et de la Technologie, University of 8 May 1945 Guelma, P.O. Box 401, 24000 Guelma, Algeria
e-mail: badrimeftah@yahoo.fr

Département des Mathématiques Faculté des mathématiques, de l'informatique et des sciences de la matiére, Université 8 mai 1945 Guelma, Algeria
e-mail: mekalfakhaoula@yahoo.com

Abstract.   In this paper, a new identity is given, some weighted trapezoidal type inequalities via h-preinvexity are established, and several known results are derived.

2020 Mathematics Subject Classification.   26D10, 26D15, 26A51.

Key words and phrases.   Hermite-Hadamard inequality, Hölder inequality, h-preinvex functions.
